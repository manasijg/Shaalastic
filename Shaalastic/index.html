About Us

Shaalastic LLP is an upcoming start-up enterprise focused on the education sector in India. In a country of a Billion plus people and with the median age of the population under 30, we believe that education as a market is untapped, unexplored and ripe of innovation. Our debut product aims at providing a comprehensive, all-in-one platform to manage the entire systems and operations that are necessary to run any educational institution. The product, tentatively titled E-ducate is aimed at launching a cloud based thin client application and a mobile application that can seamlessly connect students, teachers, non-teaching staff and parents in a homogeneous and closed ecosystem whereby critical information can be shared and learning can be imparted.

Faq Question

Will parents be able to check Student's report?

Yes, Parents would be provided separate accounts which would be connected to Student's account and they would be able to see all the reports.

How long is Free Trial Period?

E-ducate is free for any institution for the first 3 months. After the free period the pricings will vary according to the number of users.

Does E-ducate have mobile app?

Yes, E-ducate offers you service through Mobile Apps on both Android and iOS platforms.

Can we manage Accounting in E-ducate?

Yes E-ducate provides Accounting Feature which enables the Institutes to keep a track of their Expenses and also Generate Invoices.
